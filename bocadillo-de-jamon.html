Bocadillo de jamón

Description

The bocadillo de jamón can be made of jamón ibérico, jamón serrano, del país... It is very popular among students and construction workers all abroad Spain.

And blah blah blah

Ingredients

  • Jamón serrano
  • A baguette
  • Olive oil
  • If you want to try a different style, get some special catalan's spreading tomatos
  1. Start with slicing the baguette in half, all the way long
  2. Take your jamón and with your hands divide it into smaller pieces
  3. If you are feeling brave and want to try the catalan style, spread some tomatos upon the bread, in every slice. Don't forget the olive oil
  4. Add the jamón to one slice of bread
  5. Close the two halves together and enjoy your pure spanish style bocadillo de jamón.
