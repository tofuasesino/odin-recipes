Gazpacho andaluz

Description

Gazpacho is a summer traditional spanish recipe. It is made of fresh vegetables and can be eaten as a soup or drink

Some veggies included in this recipe are, for example, the garlic. So if you come to Spain and are trying to flirt with someone, you might not want to drink a glass of chilled gazpacho before the meeting.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Green pepper
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar
  1. Start with washing and peeling off all the veggies
  2. Slice every veggie into tiny pieces
  3. In a blender, mix the slices with water and blend it
  4. Fill a bottle or glass jar with the gazpacho and put it into the fridge
  5. Now you can drink your gazpacho bien fresquito.
