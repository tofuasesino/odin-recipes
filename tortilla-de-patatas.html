Tortilla de patatas

Description

The tortilla española is a very popular recipe from Spain. It is made of sliced and fried potatoes, onion and eggs.

Honestly, this recipe is the most savoury tortilla or omelette that I have ever tasted. Everytime that my mum cooks tortilla española, I become some kind of tortilla monster and I just can't stop eating.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • Big onion
  • 4/5 potatoes
  • Olive oil (very important)
  1. Peel, wash and slice the potatoes into small cubes
  2. The same with the onion
  3. In a frying pan, put a great quantity of the olive oil. Once it's hot, add the sliced potatoes
  4. When cooked, break them apart and add the onion to the pan.
  5. Beat the eggs in a bowl
  6. Add the onion and the sliced potatoes to that bowl
  7. Add the mix into a frying pan and cook it until the base is solidified
  8. Using a plate, cover the pan and rotate it until de tortilla falls upon the plate. Then, put the tortilla again to the frying pan.
